Player Blue
Player Green

INFO

Mission: destroy all enemy elementals.

During your turn, you may move your elemental and attack the enemy within the elemental's attack zone.

Your turn ends if there is no enemy in your elemantal's attack zone.

Three identical elementals arranged in a vertical, horizontal or diagonal line merge to create one stronger elemental.

The elemental's attack zone is highlited on the opponent's field. The higher the elemental's level, the larger its range. Once any high level elemental is killed, it will transform into a low level elemental.

Two pyramids are added to the last rows of the field after each turn. Pyramids can move your elemental forward.

Spells: You can only use one spell per turn. The spheres around a spell icon represent the number of elementals collected. You can cast a spell once all the spheres are glowing blue.

1) Forest Master Staff
- inflicts damage to one elemental. Avaliable after you collect 4 level 2 elementals. This powerful spell can kill 1st and 2nd level elementals instantly, and deal heavy damage to 3rd level elementals.

2) The Lady of the Lake's Vial
- fully restores an elemental's health. Avaliable after collecting 5 level 2 elementals.

3) Ancient Figurine
- inflicts damage to all elementals in vertical and horizontal lines. Avaliable after collecting 7 level 2 elementals. Place it in a free cell on the opponent's field. It kills the smallest elementals instantly and inflicts damage to all elementald in vertical and horizontal lines.

4) Pendulum of Reversed Time
- gives the player one additional move. Avaliable after collecting 9 level 2 elementals

5) Dragon Amulet
- damages all elementals in a 3x3 radius. Avaliable after collecting 10 level 2 elementals. Instantly kills all 1st and 2nd level elementals and inflicts damage on all 3rd level elementals in a 3x3 radius.
